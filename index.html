Why discover Amiens?

Amiens is a city in the north of France, crossed by the Somme. It is known for its Gothic cathedral and its nearby medieval belfry. Shops and cafes line the alleys of the Saint-Leu district. The city's canals are dotted with floating gardens, the "hortillonnages". The Picardy museum exhibits works of art and antiques over a period of several centuries. The nearby Jules Verne house is a museum where the science fiction author once lived.
